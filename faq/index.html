Marketing Material Portal FAQ

Fast answers for ordering, approvals, billing, and custom requests.

Welcome to the Marketing Material Portal

These branded portals were designed to make ordering marketing materials easier and faster for your team. Each manager is encouraged to place orders using the form found under each item.

Each order is placed using the form found under your requested item. After you place an order, you’ll receive a confirmation.

In the top search bar, type the name of the item you’re looking for.

All leave behinds, door hangers, review cards, flyers, and miscellaneous marketing materials can be ordered using your brand’s marketing material portal.

Northwest Family of Brands marketing materials include leave behinds, door hangers, flyers, coffee shop takeover cards, review cards, thank you cards, service standards, service standard posters, Northwest Way posters, and more.

Marketing materials on the portal will be coded and charged to your office by Marketing and Accounts Payable.

Invoices for print requests may be sent to your office through Yooz up to 8 weeks after your order.

Please do not create or change marketing materials containing Northwest logos, icons, or The Mouse. The Marketing team helps make sure everything reflects the trusted brand built together.

If you have a strong idea, send it to Marketing so it can be reviewed and developed the right way.

Marketing is trained and equipped to receive feedback from teammates and customers. That feedback, combined with team experience and brand history, helps create materials that are clear, concise, and aligned.

Email your feedback, requests, and ideas to marketingcommunications@callnorthwest.com and the team will explore how to make it happen.

For additional support, email marketingcommunications@callnorthwest.com.

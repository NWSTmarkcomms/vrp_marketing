Volunteer Rid-A-Pest Marketing Portal FAQ

Fast answers for ordering, approvals, billing, and custom requests.

Welcome to the Volunteer Rid-A-Pest Marketing Portal

This portal was built to make ordering marketing materials easier and faster for your team. Each manager is encouraged to place orders using the form found under each item.

Each order is placed using the form found under your requested item. After you place an order, you’ll receive a confirmation.

In the top search bar, type the name of the item you’re looking for.

All leave-behinds, door hangers, review cards, flyers, and miscellaneous marketing materials can be ordered using the Volunteer Rid-A-Pest marketing portal.

Volunteer Rid-A-Pest marketing materials include leave-behinds, door hangers, flyers, review cards, thank you cards, service materials, posters, and more.

Marketing materials on the portal will be coded and charged to your office by the appropriate internal team.

Invoices for print requests may be sent to your office after your order is submitted and processed.

Please do not create or change marketing materials containing Volunteer Rid-A-Pest logos, icons, or branded elements.

If you have a strong idea, send it in for review so it can be developed the right way.

Marketing helps protect the brand and create materials that are clear, consistent, and aligned across every location.

Email your feedback, requests, and ideas to marketing@volunteerridapest.com and the team will review your request.

For additional support, email marketing@volunteerridapest.com.
